Bread Omellete Recipie

A bread omelette is a popular, quick meal or snack that combines an omelette with bread slices.
It's made by cooking an egg mixture (with spices, onions, tomatoes, and sometimes chilies) on a pan, then placing bread
slices on top so they stick to the egg. The omelette and bread cook together, creating a filling, savory dish that’s often served with ketchup or chutney.
It’s commonly enjoyed as a street food in India and is popular for breakfast or as a light snack.

Ingredients :
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • A pinch of turmeric powder (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Oil or butter, for cooking

Instructions
  1. Prepare the Egg Mixture: In a bowl, whisk the eggs. Add chopped onion, green chili, tomato (if using), coriander leaves, salt, pepper, and a pinch of turmeric if you like. Mix well.
  2. Cook the Omelette: Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat and add a little oil or butter. Pour half of the egg mixture onto the pan, spreading it evenly.
  3. Add Bread Slices: Place the bread slices on top of the omelette mixture while it's still wet. Press gently so they stick.
  4. Flip and Cook: When the omelette is mostly cooked on one side, carefully flip it with a spatula so the bread side faces down. Let it cook until the bread is toasted and the omelette is fully done.
  5. Serve: Once golden and crispy, remove from the pan. Cut into halves if you like, and serve hot with ketchup or green chutney.
Enjoy your delisious Bread Omelette!
